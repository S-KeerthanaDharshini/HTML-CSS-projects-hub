Top three locations to visit in Tamil Nadu

Ooty — Queen of Hill Stations

A UNESCO World Heritage treasure, echoing the architectural brilliance of a thousand years past.

Thanjavur Brihadeeswarar Temple — The Timeless Chola Marvel

A misty escape nestled in the Nilgiris, where every curve unveils a postcard-worthy view.

Velankanni Church — Lourdes of the East

A serene sanctuary by the sea, drawing pilgrims with its grace, faith, and healing presence.
